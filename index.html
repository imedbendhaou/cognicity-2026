About the Workshop

Cognitive Cities — The Next Frontier of Urban Intelligence

As urban populations grow and city infrastructures become increasingly complex, cognitive cities are emerging as the next frontier of urban intelligence — environments where agentic AI systems autonomously perceive, reason, and act to optimize city operations in real time.

CogniCity 2026 brings together researchers, practitioners, and policymakers to explore how agentic AI, underpinned by digital twins, edge intelligence, and trustworthy autonomous systems, is transforming urban environments into self-managing, resilient, and human-centred ecosystems — capable of anticipating disruptions, coordinating multi-domain responses, and continuously adapting to the evolving needs of their citizens.

Submission Guidelines

Paper Format
IEEE Two-Column Format
Full papers up to 6 pages in IEEE 8.5″ × 11″ two-column format, 10pt font. All submissions must be anonymised for double-blind review — author names and affiliations must not appear anywhere in the manuscript.
